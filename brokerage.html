Employee benefit programs

Government is putting more and more pressure on the business sector to improve working conditions, to pay higher salaries & wages and provide employee benefits by passing various Acts to facilitate these changes i.e.

  • Labour Relations Act - November 1996
  • Basic Conditions of Employment Act - December 1996
  • Employment Equity Act - January 2000
  • Skills Development Act - May 2000
  • Policy Holder Protection Act - March 2001
  • Medical Aid Act & Amendments - March 2001 & December 2002
  • Domestic & Farm Labourers Act - April 2003
  • PPR & FAIS Bill - 2003 & 2004
  • Employee Benefits Act

We have taken this all into consideration and have developed various cost effective and tax efficient benefit programmes to provide for these envisaged legislation changes. We utilise various products ie: pension, provident, retirement or sinking funds etc ... depending on the needs and profile of your company.

Our innovative benefit programs have been implemented in over 500 companies in South Africa and offer:

  • Employer control
  • Flexibility
  • Inflation linked
  • Transferability
  • Life cover & Disability
  • Family funeral protection
  • Retirement provision
  • Conformity in principle to union and labour law requirements
  • No HIV testing or medicals. (Subject to certain limits)
