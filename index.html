The NK Group

The NK Group was founded in 1990 as an insurance brokerage specializing in key man life assurance for business owners/directors, group life schemes, pension and provident funds. We also have an international office located in Koblenz Germany for our international clients residing in the EU.

At the request of our growing client base the health division was formed in 1992 to provide medical aids and/or medical insurance products. For our clients that were "natural health" conscious we sourced and provided top quality health supplements which were integrated into their medical aid.

As the group expanded and our client base grew we realised that to stay ahead of the market we needed to have superior systems and so in 2005 IT Solutions was born. Boasting many completed and current projects worldwide and situated in Bangalore India, with state of the art development systems and world class programmers, eFlair Management Systems specialise in unique, customised software, designed exclusively around the client with the credo "if you can imagine it, we can create it".
